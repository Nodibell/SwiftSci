# ``SwiftOptimize``

Hyperparameter Optimization & AutoML Engine for Swift.

## Overview

`SwiftOptimize` provides automated hyperparameter tuning including Grid Search, Random Search, Bayesian Optimization, Hyperband, and Cross-Validation.
